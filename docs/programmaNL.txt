Titel-NL:	TurtleStitch: Where Code Beats Pen!

Omschrijving-NL:	To celebrate TurtleStitch’s 10th anniversary, this workshop introduces a major upgrade to two block libraries, enhancing both creative freedom and precision in machine embroidery.
